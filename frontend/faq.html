Frequently Asked Questions

How does the AI itinerary suggestion work? ▼

Our AI uses Google's Gemini model to analyze your destination, travel dates, and preferences to generate personalized activity suggestions. The AI considers popular attractions, local events, and your specific interests to create an optimized itinerary.

Is my data secure? ▼

Yes! We use industry-standard encryption for data transmission (HTTPS), password hashing with bcrypt, and secure JWT tokens for authentication. Your password is never stored in plain text, and your trip data is private to your account.

Can I print my itinerary? ▼

Absolutely! Each trip has a print button that generates a beautifully formatted, printer-friendly version of your itinerary. You can also export your trip data as JSON for backup purposes.

How is the budget calculated? ▼

The budget is automatically calculated from three sources: Activities (itinerary items), Accommodations (hotels with per-night costs multiplied by nights), and Expenses (miscellaneous costs you add). The total is updated in real-time as you add or modify items.

Do I need an API key for Gemini? ▼

If you're self-hosting, you'll need your own Gemini API key from Google AI Studio. For the hosted version at travelplanner.com, we provide the API integration as part of the Pro plan.

Can I collaborate with others on a trip? ▼

Trip collaboration is available on the Team plan. This allows multiple users to view and edit the same trip, making group travel planning seamless.

Is there a mobile app? ▼

The web app is fully responsive and works great on mobile browsers. You can also install it as a Progressive Web App (PWA) on your phone for an app-like experience with offline support for viewing cached pages.

How do I delete my account? ▼

You can request account deletion by emailing support@travelplanner.com. We'll permanently delete all your data within 30 days of your request.
